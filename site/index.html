About NYC Fix It Shop

Our mission is to reduce waste, contribute to a sustainable economy, and bring New Yorkers together to learn, share, and repair. We draw inspiration from Sesame Street’s Fix It Shop and are guided by the Right to Repair and Repair Café movements. We’re laying the groundwork now for our physical space and hope to open in lower Manhattan in Spring 2025.

How It Works

Repair Services

Bring your broken items to the shop. If repaired, you can buy them back at a fair price or leave them to be sold in the store.

Repair Academy

Learn hands-on repair skills through classes for youth and community members, led by skilled mentors.

Community Repair Network

We are teaming up with local repair shops to strengthen the repair ecosystem. By working together, we’ll make it easier for you to fix what you own.
