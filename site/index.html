About NYC Fix It Shop

Our mission is to reduce waste, contribute to a sustainable economy, and bring New Yorkers together to learn, share, and repair. We draw inspiration from Sesame Street's Fix It Shop and are guided by the Right to Repair and Repair Caf√© movements. We're laying the groundwork now for our nonprofit brick-and-mortar shop in lower Manhattan.

How It Works

Repair Services

Bring your broken items to the shop. If repaired, you can buy them back at a fair price or leave them to be sold in the store.

Repair Academy

Learn hands-on repair skills through classes for youth and community members, led by skilled mentors.

Community Repair Network

Strengthening the repair ecosystem by working with repair experts to make it easier for New Yorkers to fix what they own.
