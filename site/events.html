NYC Fix It Shop - First Meeting & Get Together

We hope you will join us for the launch of NYC Fix It Shop - an initiative dedicated to repairing broken items, teaching hands-on skills, and bringing our community together around sustainable living. We've been planning and talking with lots of people for many months and are excited to share our vision with our trusted and beloved community of colleagues, friends and family.

This will be a party - with delicious homemade food and great people! Our one ask of you is that you share with us a photo of something in your home that is broken which you can't bear to throw away and also can't figure out how to get it repaired.
